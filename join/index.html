Join the Amherst Community Band!

The Amherst Community Band welcomes musicians of different ages and abilities.

Who can join

The Amherst Community Band welcomes musicians of all abilities and ages, from enthusiastic amateurs to professionals, music teachers, middle and high school students, college students, and retirees.

Membership is open to all who are interested and no audition is required.

Rehearsal information

Come play with us. Simply join a rehearsal listed on our members page.

Musician signup

Interested in playing with the Amherst Community Band? Please complete our musician signup form. Someone from the band will follow up after your submission.

Your information will be used only to respond to your interest in playing with the Amherst Community Band.

What happens next

For now, the easiest way to get started is to attend a rehearsal and introduce yourself to a band member or conductor.

If you have questions about playing with us, the members page is the best place to start.
