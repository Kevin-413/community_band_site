Amherst Community Band Website Privacy Policy (Draft)
Effective date: June 13, 2026

This draft policy explains how Amherst Community Band ("ACB," "we," "our," or "us") handles information collected through this website.

1) Information We Collect
We may collect information that you provide directly in the Contact popup form, including your name, email address, and message content.

2) How We Use Information
We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, provide requested information, and communicate about Amherst Community Band activities.

3) Third-Party Services and Embedded Content
This site may use third-party services and embeds, including Formspree (contact form processing), YouTube (video embeds), Google Maps links, and Google Calendar embeds. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

4) Cookies and Analytics
This site does not currently run a separate analytics platform controlled by ACB. Embedded third-party services may set cookies or similar technologies.

5) Data Sharing
We do not sell personal information. We share information only as needed with service providers that operate site features (for example, contact form processing).

6) Data Retention
Contact submissions are retained for as long as reasonably needed to respond and maintain communication records, unless a longer period is required by law.

7) Your Choices
You may choose not to submit contact information. If you contact us and want your message deleted, request removal using the contact information below.

8) Contact
For privacy questions or requests, please use the site contact channel shown in the footer (Webmaster link) or the Contact popup.

9) Policy Updates
We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will reflect the latest revision.
